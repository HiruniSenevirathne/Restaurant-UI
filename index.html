Discover Our Story


Waffle
is a place for simplicity.
Simple is the name of the game,
and
we are finding it in the right places,
even
in your dining experience.
We're a small group from Kandy, Sri Lanka
who
make simple food possible since 1995.
Come join us and see what simplicity tastes like.

Our Bread


We love the smell of fresh baked bread. Each loaf is handmade at the crack of daw, using only the simplest of ingredients to bring out smells and flavours that backon the whole block. Stop by anytime and experience simplicity at it's finest






Our Beer


Here at Waffle we are all about the love of beer. New and bold flavors enter our doors every week, and we can't help but show them off. While we enjoy the classics, we're always passionate about discovering something new, so stop by and experience our craft at it's best.
